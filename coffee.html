Our History

Coffee is much more than a drink—it’s a tradition, a ritual, and a passion that has spanned centuries and continents. The origins of coffee trace back to the ancient highlands of Ethiopia, where it is said that a goat herder named Kaldi first discovered the energizing effects of the coffee berry. Over the centuries, this simple discovery blossomed into a global phenomenon, sparking trade, cultural exchange, and the creation of the thriving coffee culture we know today.

Today, we continue to innovate while respecting tradition, offering everything from classic espresso to creative blends that reflect the ever-evolving landscape of coffee culture.

Join us in celebrating this timeless tradition, one perfect cup at a time.
